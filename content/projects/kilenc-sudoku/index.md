Sudoku that respects your time, your eyes, and your data. Kilenc never
makes a network request; saved games live in a JSON file on the device
and nothing else.

- **Three ways to play** — a gentle Beginner ramp with helpers on,
  Standard across Easy through Expert, and Custom where you dial in
  clues, max technique tier, and intensity yourself. Every puzzle is
  guaranteed to have exactly one solution.
- **Per-tile scoring with medals** — faster placements score more, a
  finisher bonus rewards the last minute, and a stats sheet tracks best
  score, streaks, and time invested.
- **Hints that teach, not spoil** — tap Hint and Kilenc names the
  technique to apply ("Naked Single", "XYZ-Wing") instead of just
  filling the cell. 11 human-style solving techniques across four
  tiers, with an interactive How to Play tab.
- **Built natively per platform** — SwiftUI on iOS, Jetpack Compose on
  Android, laid out for tablets so the board breathes instead of
  stretching. Light, Dark, or follow the system.

No ads, no accounts, no tracking, no analytics — just the puzzle.
