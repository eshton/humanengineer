All your pharma intelligence in one platform. From patents to approvals
to financials — faster, predictive insights for strategy and business
development teams. Tracking 6,400+ pharmaceutical companies, 6,200+
drugs, 6,000+ brand names, and 120,000+ active clinical trials.

- **AI search, pharma-trained** — one smart search across critical
  pharmaceutical data. Ask for a drug or company name and instantly see
  PDUFA dates, patents, trials, approvals, and forecasts. Built on
  trusted sources, no manual research trawl.
- **Predictive trigger engine** — an instant signal layer that turns raw
  data into signals on patent cliffs, trial delays, and pipeline gaps
  before they become policy changes. Spot risks early, plan with
  complete intelligence.
- **Insights where you work** — delivered inside Salesforce, Copilot,
  Power BI, and ChatGPT, the tools teams already use. Zero learning
  curve, no new logins, no platform switching.
- **Built for strategy and BD teams** — monitor competitors, benchmark
  portfolios, compare risk scores and pipeline impact, all without
  leaving existing workflows.

Currently running an early-access pilot program with discounted beta
pricing, dedicated onboarding, and priority access to new alerts.
