Pass the device. Take turns placing X and O on an endless field. Make
five in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — and the win seals
into a little metaball-style amoeba, tinted by whoever won it, that
stays on the board forever.

- **Hot-seat, no setup** — no accounts, no servers, no ads, no network.
  Just two humans and a board.
- **Endless field** — pinch to dive in, pull back to take it all in.
  The board doesn't end; every won round leaves an amoeba behind and
  the next round starts somewhere fresh.
- **Organic trophies** — a winning row freezes into an organic blob
  colored by the winner, building a menagerie of amoebas across an
  infinite field over time.
- **Same game everywhere** — iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV. Touch and
  pinch on iOS; touchpad cursor and Select to place on tvOS.

The ruleset is tiny; the strategy — blocking, double-threats,
sacrifice — is not. No accounts, no ads, no network calls: scores and
amoebas live only on the device.
